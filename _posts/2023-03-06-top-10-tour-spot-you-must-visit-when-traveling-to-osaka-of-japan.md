Are you planning to visit Osaka, Japan? You’re in for a treat! Osaka is known for its vibrant nightlife, delicious food, and unique attractions. From the iconic Osaka Castle to the bustling shopping districts, there is something for everyone in this city. Here is a list of the top 10 tour spots you must visit when traveling to Osaka of Japan. 

## 1. Osaka Castle
Address: 1-1 Osakajo, Chuo Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 540-0002, Japan

Osaka Castle is one of the most iconic landmarks in Osaka. The castle was built in the 16th century and is a symbol of the city’s rich history. The castle grounds are open to the public and offer beautiful views of the city. There are also many museums and exhibitions inside the castle walls. 

## 2. Dotonbori
Address: Dotonbori, Chuo Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 542-0071, Japan

Dotonbori is a popular shopping and entertainment district in Osaka. The area is known for its bright neon lights and bustling atmosphere. You can find a variety of restaurants, bars, and shops in the area. It is also home to the iconic Glico Man sign, which is a symbol of Osaka. 

## 3. Universal Studios Japan
Address: 2-1-33 Sakurajima, Konohana Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 554-0031, Japan

Universal Studios Japan is a popular theme park in Osaka. The park is home to a variety of attractions, shows, and rides. It is also home to the Wizarding World of Harry Potter, which is a must-see for any Harry Potter fan. 

## 4. Tennoji Zoo
Address: 1-108 Chausuyamacho, Tennoji Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 543-0063, Japan

Tennoji Zoo is a popular zoo in Osaka. The zoo is home to a variety of animals, including tigers, elephants, and giraffes. There are also many interactive exhibits and activities for visitors to enjoy. 

## 5. Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan
Address: 1-1-10 Kaigandori, Minato Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 552-0022, Japan

Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan is one of the largest aquariums in the world. The aquarium is home to a variety of marine life, including sharks, rays, and jellyfish. There are also many interactive exhibits and activities for visitors to enjoy. 

## 6. Sumiyoshi Taisha
Address: 2-9-89 Sumiyoshihonmachi, Sumiyoshi Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 558-0045, Japan

Sumiyoshi Taisha is one of the oldest shrines in Japan. The shrine is known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere. It is also home to many festivals throughout the year. 

## 7. Kuromon Ichiba Market
Address: 2-4-1 Nipponbashi, Chuo Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 542-0073, Japan

Kuromon Ichiba Market is a popular market in Osaka. The market is known for its fresh seafood and produce. You can find a variety of restaurants and shops in the area. It is also home to many unique souvenirs and gifts. 

## 8. Umeda Sky Building
Address: 1-1-88 Oyodonaka, Kita Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 530-0012, Japan

Umeda Sky Building is a popular skyscraper in Osaka. The building is home to a variety of restaurants, shops, and attractions. You can also take an elevator to the top of the building and enjoy the stunning views of the city. 

## 9. Osaka Science Museum
Address: 4-2-1 Nakanoshima, Kita Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 530-0005, Japan

Osaka Science Museum is a popular museum in Osaka. The museum is home to a variety of interactive exhibits and activities. You can also find a planetarium and an observatory in the museum. 

## 10. Shinsaibashi Shopping Arcade
Address: 1-7-6 Shinsaibashisuji, Chuo Ward, Osaka, Osaka Prefecture 542-0085, Japan

Shinsaibashi Shopping Arcade is a popular shopping district in Osaka. The area is home to a variety of shops, restaurants, and bars. You can find a variety of souvenirs and gifts in the area.
